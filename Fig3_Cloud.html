Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Analysis

This scatter plot takes a pole to pole view of Planet Earth. The X-axis represents the fraction of the sky obscured by clouds in cities around the world. Cloudiness percent is farely evenly distributed across latitudes and weighted toward the ends at 0% and 100%.
